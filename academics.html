Our Strategic Focus

The strategic focus for West Walk Secondary Schools (Idimu, Lekki, Victoria Garden City, Ikeja, Abuja and Festac Area)

  • Developing and improving the creative and innovative abilities of students and teachers.
  • Expanding the level of science and mathematics teaching and learning.
  • Implementing an information science and technology culture.
  • Emphasizing scientific sport development
  • Fostering an entrepreneurship culture.
  • Instilling positive cultural life values and discipline in the teaching and learning programme.
  • Raising the academic excellence and performance of students.
  • Developing the level of human capital (via knowledge, skills and attitude training) of students and teachers.
  • Managing the quality of the teaching and learning programmes in the schools.
  • Contributing to a culture of life-long learning amongst students and teachers.

Our Model of Excellence

  • Quality of leadership
  • Development of strategies and policies
  • Clear client and market focus
  • Resources and information management
  • Client satisfaction
  • Student achievements

Regarding the latter, the achievement of West Walk Schools cannot be over-emphasized.
Academically, our students have recorded a 100% pass in the last four years in national and

international examinations, a testament to our ranking as one of the best schools in Nigeria.

Awards have also been won in co-curricular activities within and outside Lagos
and the college is well known to have won awards in sporting activities.

West Walk High School Victoria Garden City has won the Science Olympiad and was awarded
the Merit Award presented by the Director General National Mathematical Centre,
as well as the 8th Essay Writing Competition presented by the Nigerian Stock Exchange.
